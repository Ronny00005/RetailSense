Retail Sense is a sales and retail analytics platform that transforms raw CSV sales data into meaningful insights, dashboards, and future trend predictions. It helps businesses make data-driven decisions without requiring technical expertise.

Retail store owners, sales analysts, inventory managers, business students, and anyone who needs to analyze sales data can benefit from Retail Sense. No technical background is required.

üìÑ CSV & Data

The system supports CSV (Comma Separated Values) files containing structured sales data. Your CSV file should include columns for date, product name, quantity, and revenue.

If the CSV format is incorrect or missing required columns, the system will notify you with specific error messages. You'll be prompted to upload a valid file with the correct structure.

Currently, the system can handle CSV files up to 50MB in size. This typically accommodates several years of sales data for most retail businesses.
